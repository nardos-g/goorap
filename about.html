About Us

Who we Are?

GooRap is a company that provides delivery, courier, and logistics services. It is set up to make logistics movement easier in Ethiopia. We intend to operate in various countries around the world. It will ship various items, including documents, laboratory specimens, and results. It has branch offices all over Ethiopia. We offer a variety of service options, which are clearly listed on our services page.

What we are about?

We deliver packages, documents, medical laboratory specimens, and test results. We are going to make logistics transportation easier.

How we go about things?

We have a motorcycle, a truck, and a van available for shipping. We use planes to transport our express freight cargo. Within the city, motorcycles are used for order pickup and delivery. Outside of cities, we use trucks and vans to provide our standard services. Regional representatives are available. They collect orders from all over Ethiopia and deliver them to the designated location. These agents receive shipments from other parts of Ethiopia and deliver them to the intended recipient.
